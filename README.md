# Bluecon - Aquaculture Management System

A simple aquaculture farm management system for tracking fish breeding operations from hatchery to delivery.

## Overview

Bluecon helps fish farms manage their breeding operations, track costs, and fulfill customer orders efficiently. 

## Features

- **Farm Management**: Multi-farm support with tank monitoring and water quality tracking
- **Species & Batches**: Track fish groups from birth to shipment with profit margins
- **Cost Tracking**: Monitor feed, labor, utilities, and medication costs per batch
- **Health Monitoring**: Log feeding schedules, mortality, and water parameters
- **Order Management**: Process customer orders and manage shipments

## Database Structure

- **Master Data**: Species, farms, and tanks
- **Operations**:  Batches, costs, feeding, and health logs  
- **Sales**:  Customers, orders, and shipments


## Benefits

- Complete cost visibility per batch
- Streamlined operations across multiple farms
- Efficient order processing and delivery tracking
- Health and environmental monitoring
